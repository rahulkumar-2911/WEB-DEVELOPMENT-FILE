Div and Span

This is a 'div'

A `div` is a block-level container. We use it to group this heading and paragraph into one "card" with a border and background.

This is another 'div'

It starts on its own new line.

A `span` is an inline element. We use it to style just a few words inside a paragraph without starting a new line. It just wraps the text that is inside it.
